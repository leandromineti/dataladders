# Data Ladders

> Work in progress.

A career ladder[^1] is a document defining the sequence of roles a professional can assume in its career within an organization. This is a critical document in people management, working as a background for many processes such as workforce planning, hiring, training and performance assessment.

## Acknowledgements

This repository is inspired by [engineering ladders](https://github.com/jorgef/engineeringladders), an interesting open career framework focused on software engineering. For those wanting to go further on the subjects, a recommended list of materials and readings is available on [references](./references.md).

[^1]: also known as career progression framework, career pathway or career track.
